Into Worship Services

Introduction

Worship is a time to exalt God and unite as one community of faith. It is not just a ritual but a living encounter with the Lord, where we bring our hearts in praise, prayer, and surrender.

📖 "Come, let us bow down in worship, let us kneel before the Lord our Maker." – Psalm 95:6

Schedule & Location

  • When: Every Friday, 7:00 PM – 9:30 PM
  • Where: Main Sanctuary
  • Online Worship: ▶ Watch Online on YouTube

Order of Worship

  1. Praise & Prayer
  2. Sermon
  3. Praise & Prayer
  4. Benediction

Worship Teams & Volunteers

  • Praise Team (Battle Cry): A passionate worship community of the young generation, leading the church in heartfelt praise and adoration.
  • Media Team: Serving behind the scenes with sound, visuals, and livestream support to ensure worship is experienced both onsite and online.
