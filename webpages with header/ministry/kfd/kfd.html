KFD

Korean Food Day

Our Mission

KFD is not simply a small festival or event organized by foreigners. Its purpose is to share IM USA's mission and vision, encourage people to join in, and ultimately, create disciples who become like Jesus.

Culture & Faith

Through authentic Korean cuisine and traditional games, we bridge cultures and share God's love. Every dish tells a story, every gathering builds community, and every moment celebrates the diversity of God's kingdom.

Building Community

From welcoming to worship services, KFD creates spaces where hearts connect, friendships bloom, and the gospel spreads naturally through genuine relationships and shared experiences.
